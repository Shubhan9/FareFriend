About FareFriend

Your trusted companion for fair auto-rickshaw fares in Mumbai

Our Mission

FareFriend was created with a simple goal: to empower commuters with accurate fare information and promote transparency in auto-rickshaw travel. We believe that everyone deserves to know the fair price for their journey.

Accurate Calculations

Using real-time traffic data and official fare rates to provide the most accurate estimates possible.

Community Driven

Powered by user reports and feedback to maintain up-to-date fare information and identify fare discrepancies.

Smart Navigation

Intelligent route calculation considering traffic patterns and common shortcuts taken by auto-rickshaws.

Consumer Protection

Helping you avoid overcharging with fare breakdowns and meter tampering warnings.

How It Works

FareFriend uses a sophisticated algorithm that considers multiple factors including:

  • Official RTO-approved fare rates
  • Real-time traffic conditions
  • Time of day and peak hours
  • User-reported actual fares
  • Historical travel patterns

Community Impact

Our platform helps create a more transparent and fair transport ecosystem by:

  • Reducing fare disputes between passengers and drivers
  • Identifying problematic routes and areas with frequent overcharging
  • Helping users make informed decisions about their travel
  • Building a database of reliable fare information

The Team

FareFriend is developed and maintained by a team of passionate developers and urban transport enthusiasts who believe in making city commuting more transparent and hassle-free.
